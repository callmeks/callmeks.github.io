## Five Star Feedback

```
- Difficulty: 2 star
- Category: Broken Access Control
- Description: Get rid of all 5-star customer feedback.
```

### Method to solve

- According to the challenge and the description, we are required to delete all 5 star feedback given by customers.
- To solve this challenge, it will requires us to access administration page of the website.
<br>
![](/assets/img/posts/2023-05-09-OWASP-Juice-Shop-Five-Star-Feedback/2023-05-09-OWASP-Juice-Shop-Five-Star-Feedback-19-07-58.png)
<br>
- According to the administration page, there are some customer feedback with 5 star.
- Remove all feedback that contains 5 star with the icon beside each feedback and the challenge will be considered solved.
